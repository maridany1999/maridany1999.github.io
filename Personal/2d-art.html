Traditional Art

Many of these drawings were created during my teenage years. Most are original compositions, while others were inspired by various sources, and few were drawn with reference images with the purpose to learn how to draw. While I can't recall the specific details of every piece, each one has a lot of personal meaning. Hope you enjoy them!

Any fan art (e.g. Harry Potter, etc.) or inspired works were created as learning exercises and for personal inspiration only. No commercial use intended. This sub section serves as a personal portfolio to showcase my drawings, as a hobby, and its learning process.
